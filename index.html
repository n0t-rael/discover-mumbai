History of Mumbai

Mumbai was originally seven islands. It was ruled by different empires before coming under British rule. Over time land reclamation joined the islands into one large city. Today Mumbai is India's financial and entertainment capital.

Why is Mumbai Called the City of Dreams?

People from across India come to Mumbai for jobs, business, Bollywood, and opportunities. It is a city where dreams become reality through hard work.
